Turin baroque music festival

2025-10-25

The 4th edition of the baroque music festival organized by Accademia del Santo Spirito, which took place in June–July and October–November 2025 in some of the most beautiful churches and palaces in Torino.

These pictures and videos are from the following concerts:

  • 21 October: Palestrina 500 at Real Chiesa di San Lorenzo
  • 25 October: Barocco tedesco at Villa della regina
  • 28 October: La scuola violinistica piemontese at Chiesa dello Spirito Santo
  • 4 November: Te Deum at Cattedrale di San Giovanni Battista

with some bonus content from the equally magnificent Advent/Christmas music festival which followed it:

  • 15 November: Preludio Ensemble e Compositori Associati at Chiesa di San Rocco
  • 20 November: Mythos e Corale Roberto Goitre at Cappella dei Mercanti
