It's been a while since I last published some music. I'm still playing all the time, especially in the past couple of months, but I'm doing so many things, musically and otherwise, that there had not been anything I managed to study and play well enough to merit a post.

But today I have something very unusual, in many ways: a composition of my own. This is the first time I put something I wrote here (at least officially, and even though almost every arrangement I create is in some way a composition of mine). This piece was born in… very unusual circumstances, to say the least. I won't go into much detail — it's for the best, trust me —, but I will say as a bit of context that it's a short piece meant as an introductory soundtrack for a fictional story. It's named incomunicabilità (it was baptized by an external entity, not by me).

Lastly, as a result of the aforementioned activities which have been keeping me busy, everything has gotten better, except for the playing, so enjoy this new high-quality overhead camera setup.
